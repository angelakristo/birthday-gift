A message for you

Today is your day, but honestly, every day I feel grateful that you exist.

You are not only my sister. You are the person who means the most to me in this world. I love you and respect you more than words can explain. Without you, so many things in my life would not make sense.

Thank you for everything you have done for me, for everything you have sacrificed, and especially for the time you gave me. I know that sometimes it may seem like I do not notice everything, but I do. I notice more than you think, and I am deeply, deeply grateful.

Thank you for choosing to be by my side in moments when I needed you the most, even when you could have been somewhere else, starting your own life in Amsterdam with your partner, away from responsibilities and worries. Instead, you were there for me. With me. Supporting me, guiding me, protecting me, and loving me in ways I will never forget.

And looking at the world we live in, a world where siblings often grow apart as they get older, I feel even more grateful for what we have. Because with us, it has never been like that. The more we grow, the more we love each other, respect each other, and need each other. And that is something I will never take for granted.

You are a huge part of who I am today. You are part of my upbringing, part of my heart, and one of the biggest reasons I keep becoming a better version of myself. You make me a better person just by being who you are.

It is impossible to describe with words how much this world needs people like you. People with your heart, your strength, your kindness, your patience, and your love. I hope you know how rare you are.

And more than anything, I hope that one day I become even half of the person you are, in every part of life. Because to me, you are not just my sister. You are one of the greatest examples of what a good person should be.

I hope this birthday reminds you how loved, appreciated, and irreplaceable you are. You deserve the whole world, and even that would not be enough.

Happy birthday to the best sister and one of the best people in the world. 💗
